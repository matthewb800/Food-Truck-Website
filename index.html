What is Tropical Twist?

Our mission is to offer vibrant and healthy Acai bowls
and smoothies to your everyday meals, packed with nutrient-rich
ingredients that make you feel as good as they taste.

Whether you’re grabbing a quick breakfast, a post-workout
boost, or a refreshing treat, we’re here to make your experience
delightful and satisfying.

Come visit us at our food truck or explore our menu online
– we can't wait to share our love for acai bowls and smoothies with you!
